# 17.2.1 Worked Ecosystem Scenarios

_Page Type: Worked Example | Maturity: Outline_

This subsection turns the chapter into concrete organizational situations so the abstractions can be checked against real delivery contexts.

## Cloud-estate concentration

A company realizes its AI dependence sits more with one cloud ecosystem than with one specific model vendor.

- What this example makes visible: maps the ecosystem actors behind applied AI systems and distinguishes organizations from the products, models, and standards they influence.
- What to watch for: The failure mode is only tracking direct model-provider risk.

## Open ecosystem adoption

- What to watch for: The failure mode is assuming open code means low strategic dependence.
