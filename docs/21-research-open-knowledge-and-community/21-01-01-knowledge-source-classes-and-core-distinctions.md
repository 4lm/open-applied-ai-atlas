## Why These Distinctions Matter

The practical question is not whether the labels are elegant. The practical question is whether the distinctions change how a team designs, reviews, buys, operates, or audits a system. In this chapter, that means checking whether the topic is being reasoned about in the right layer and with the right cross-cutting lenses still visible.

## Key Lenses

| Public-knowledge lens | Why it matters |
| --- | --- |
| Source type | Separates research, open-source docs, public guidance, and community maps |
| Practical value | Shows which resources help with implementation versus orientation |
| Neutrality and bias | Makes vendor influence or community limits visible |
| Maintenance posture | Keeps old but popular resources from being treated as current truth |

## What Matters Most

- Public knowledge is fragmented and must be read selectively
- Open-source documentation is often the fastest route to implementation detail
- Neutral public guidance is valuable but often higher level
- Curated maps and community resources age quickly and need maintenance awareness
