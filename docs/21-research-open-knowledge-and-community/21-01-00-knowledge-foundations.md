## What This Section Helps Decide

- whether the source in front of the team is evidence, implementation guidance, community operating knowledge, or ecosystem noise
- what the source can legitimately influence: orientation, shortlist work, pilot design, control updates, or release evidence
- when a source-quality question is actually a taxonomy, evaluation, security, standards, or sourcing question in another chapter
- which review triggers should fire before public knowledge becomes internal policy or platform default
