## What This Section Helps Decide

- whether the topic belongs in an applied organizational AI and ML atlas at all, or whether it is really pure research commentary, legal advice, or vendor positioning
- which readers need to align first before architecture, governance, privacy, sourcing, or operations discussions start using different assumptions
- whether the system under review is being framed too narrowly as GenAI convenience instead of a broader mix of predictive, retrieval, perception, optimization, or hybrid organizational systems
- which recurring priorities must stay explicit even when later chapters focus on models, gateways, deployment, evaluation, security, oversight, or sourcing
